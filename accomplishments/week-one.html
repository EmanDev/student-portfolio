Week 1 Accomplishments

This page contains the work done for the first week of our On-the-job training at DMCI Masbate Power Corporation. You can check out the summary at the end of this page.

Week 1 is comprised of sets of tours and field trainings within the power plant. Base from our Internship Work Plan, our first week of training are for plant operation orientation, central control room, and a selected field (boiler/turbine/demi-plant & ecp operations).

In my case, the field I have selected is the Demi-Plant & Electro-Chlorination Plant Operations as it has a vital role in running the thermal power plant smoothly without any hiccups.



Overview

Before we jump to the main part of this page, let us first describe each mentioned training done this week.

Power Plant Operation Orientation

  • Orientation of plant operating system where trainees are subjected to power plant tour guided by the assigned mentor.
  • The trainees will be visiting each part of the site to get an overview of how things are done within the power plant.
  • They are also introduced to their fellow trainees, seniors, and mentors.

Central Control Room

  • Familiarization on thermal power plant system where trainees are oriented and taught about the operations of boiler, turbine, and the overall balance of systems within the power plant.
  • The trainees will be visiting each part of the power plant to understand the actual process of each system in the power plant.
  • The trainees will be observing each system in the CCR and compare it to their actual observation during their tour/visits.

Demi-Plant & ECP Operations

  • Demi-Plant refers to demineralization plant; it is a desalination plant where seawater is converted into demineralized water to be used on the power plants' system (i.e. for generating steam and for the cooling system of different power plant auxiliaries).
  • The trainees will be visiting the demi-plant to understand how seawater is converted into demineralized water.
  • The trainees will be visiting the laboratory to understand the process of water sampling and assist on laboratory activities.


Field & General Training

The students are deployed to each stations. As for my field training, I was stationed at Demi-Plant & Electro-Chlorination Plant.


• Power Plant Operation Orientation •

June 30, 2022 - First day of our on-the-job training. We met Engr. Bernard, a BSEE graduate from Divine Word College of Legazpi. He gave us an overview of thermal and diesel power plant while roaming around the site. In particular, we get to visit the main thermal power plants' parts and systems (i.e. turbine, condenser, generator, furnace, steam drum, slag cooler, electrostatic precipitator - ESP, deaerator and such). We were delighted to see how each part and department within the DMCI Masbate Power Corporation (DMPC) site works in order to provide stable and sufficient electrical energy supply in the province of Masbate (particularly the 2nd and 3rd district of the province).


• Central Control Room •

July 1 & July 4-6, 2022 - We were oriented about the purpose of several components stored in the Distributed Control System as well as the Central Control Room's necessary power supply and control. The mentioned rooms contain different kinds of power supply connection, necessary for the function of the power plant, which is in both AC and DC (DC is used in case of power interruption or during power plant shutdown/preventive maintenance). Several circuit breaker racks are also used for the power plant's overall protection, which are also stationed at different parts of the site. We were toured by our supervisor Engr. Joebert and Engr. Niño.


• Demi-Plant & Electro-Chlorination Plant Operations - Solo Field Training•

July 6-7, 2022 - I was stationed, solo, at Demi-Plant or also called as Demi-Water Treatment Plant. I was supervised by Sir "Giorg" or "Toto" during my field. He taught me the process flow of how seawater is desalinized/demineralized into pure water. During the field, I was able to understand the things he taught and was able to conduct meter recordings in the different parts of the water treatment plant.

• Week 1 Accomplishment Summary •

Too Long, Didn't Read? Here's a summary of the things I/we did during the past week. I thank all my mentors and peers at work for guiding each one of us. We've learned so much about the different functions and processes within the power plant. We have also made some friends along the way!

Date/s Activities Output Skills Learned Tools Used Remarks
June 30, 2022 Overview of plant operating system Able to understand how power plant supplied power to the transmission and distribution line and household I am able to understand how the power plant generated electrical energy to be supplied and consumed by consumers of electric utility company through transmission and distribution lines. Notebook and pen

Engr. Bernard, our mentor/supervisor today, was very considerate towards us.

He answers all our question and gave us things to remember about the things he taught to us.
July 1, 2022 Familiarization on thermal plant system (boiler system, turbine system, and balance of plant) Able to understand the function of all main auxillary equipment in all systems

I've learned the simple process of how the thermal power plant generate electricity.

Considering all essential resources like water and coal are loaded and running on the system, the coal is delivered and used as a fuel in the furnace to boil water and generate steam.

 Computer, notebook, pen and radio intercom

All of our supervisors inside the control room were pretty chill.

For the second tour, Engr. Joebert, Engr. Niño, and others were also pretty active on teaching us and our group exchanged some words with them including things like introduction, questions, and answers.
July 4, 2022 Familiarization on turbine system of thermal power plant Able to understand basic functions of turbine system and generator in generating the required load demand

I've learned that the turbine revolves at 4860 RPM and the generator, made and assembled by the japanese, runs at 1800 RPM at 60hz.

With a reduction gear, the generator was able to revolve at 1800 RPM with the help of the turbine revolving at 4860 RPM, giving us 62.963% reduced speed.

 Computer, notebook and pen Sir Cervantes was very kind and very passionate at teaching us.
July 5, 2022 Familiarization on boiler system of thermal power plant Able to understand basic functions of boiler system in order to successfully operate the thermal power plant

I've learned that the boiler system has different parts which plays a vital role in keeping the thermal power plant in an normal operating condition.

Some of the parts of the boiler system shows how the overall system operates in a very efficient manner by reusing heat from heat-losses and saving fuel (coal).

 Notebook and pen Sir Joe and Sir France were very kind and considerate towards us in teaching about the boiler system and letting us watch/observe the actual vital parts of the system.
July 6, 2022 Familiarization of processes within the demi-plant Able to understand how seawater is converted into demi-water

I've learned that there are 6 steps/stages in demineralizing seawater

Those are through clarifier, multimedia filter, active carbon filter, Primary & Secondary Reverse Osmosis, and Electrodeionization (EDI).

 Notebook and pen Sir Toto was very passionate in teaching me and told be about some stuff on how he handle things during operation.
