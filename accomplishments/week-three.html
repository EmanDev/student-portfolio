Week 3 Accomplishments

This page contains the work done for the third week of our On-the-job training at DMCI Masbate Power Corporation. You can check out the summary at the end of this page.

Week 3 is comprised of sets of operation trainings in thermal and diesel power plant. Base from our Internship Work Plan, our third week of training are for the continuation of field training and control & operations training at the diesel power plant.



Overview

Before we jump to the main part of this page, let us first describe each mentioned training done this week.

Boiler System

  • Continuation of previous field training tasks.
  • Boiler system tour, inspection, and familiarization of each parts.
  • Coal handling operations, radio-ing, motor-control switches, and monitoring.

Extra: Peer-to-Peer Teaching

  • Taught basic process flow of thermal power plant from demi-plant > boiler system > turbine system > generator.
  • Taught basic load distribution process.

Control: Diesel Power Plant

  • Diesel engine description: 2 Diesel Engines - Nigata 1 (sick-leave) & Nigata 2 (functional at 5MW max).
  • Familiarize the process of load distribution from thermal power plant + diesel power plant to NPC then MASELCO.
  • Familiarize terms regarding the process of simple load dispatching.
  • Learn generator synchronization and distribution of loads between 2 power plants or generators (Base vs Island).


Operation and General Training

The students are deployed to diesel power plant station. Training includes understanding load dispatching, load distribution, diesel control functions & fuel module system usage, and many others.


• Boiler System •

Continuation of previous field training tasks at boiler system. We roam around the thermal power plant, inspected and recorded performance of ash hoppers, ESP, silos, chambers, and many others. We also conducted site inspection on nearby devices used by the said system which includes circuit breaker racks at the distribution room, coal handling conveyers and coal crushers.


• Diesel Power Plant Operations •

We familiarize all topics assigned to us during our stay at the diesel power plant control (4 days). We've talked about the experiences of each assigned operator during their shifts, asked about how they operate and maintain both Nigata diesel engines. Lastly, we communicated, via text message and messenger, with NPC, MASELCO, and plant supervisors with regards to the performance (status of load and such) of both thermal and diesel power plant.
