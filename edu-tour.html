Electrical Engineering Education Tour 2023

The EEducational Tour 2023 was conducted on March 20-26, 2023. The tour provided a fair way in gaining knowledge and idea in the field. During our tour, we visited several destinations that was able to give me insights and set new goals for myself in the near future.

The above illustration provide an idea on where did we go, as a group, during the tour. Around the area outlined above includes the particulars about power plants, electrical manufacturing plants, research and development centers, electrical safety trainings, and renewable energy facilities. By visiting these places and participating in activities during the tour, I was able to gain a deeper understanding of the principles and fields of electrical engineering, the practical applications of electrical technologies, and the various career opportunities available in this field.

Please help yourselves, you can open the Menu in the top-left corner of this page then access the navigation for the sites visited or choose a destination you want to visit below. You can also access others sites I visited during our Educational Tour for more information. Check out power plants or the preliminaries.
