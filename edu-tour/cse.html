CSE Power, FCIE, Dasmariñas, Cavite

In 1978, CHUAN SHUN ELECTRIC INDUSTRIAL COMPANY (CSE Power) originated from Taiwan and subsequently expanded its business to the Philippines in 2010. The company produces a range of products such as Dry type power transformer, AVR transformer, Automobile transformer, High-frequency transformer, Three-phase transformer with a maximum capacity of 1MVA, Coils, Reactor, Air choke, and Wire products.

Operation and Design

CSE Power Philippines is situated in the First Cavite Industrial Estate in Dasmariñas, Cavite, covering an area of 5000 square meters. The manufacturing of transformers involves intricate and challenging processes, so the company has more than 700 skilled workers to guarantee product quality. The laborers manually assemble the products using imported raw materials. To meet customer demands, they aim to produce 200 finished products per hour. The plant has designated stations, each with their own specific tasks. During the manufacturing process, they use chemicals such as paint and varnish. However, the factory lacks proper ventilation, making the work environment unsafe.

Procedures

Transformers play a vital role in power systems as they convert electrical voltage from one level to another, and they come in different shapes and sizes based on their application. During the factory visit, the supervisor in charge of the product explained the process of manufacturing power transformers that are primarily utilized in electronic devices.

  • Creating a transformer design based on customer requirements.
  • Procuring the necessary raw materials, such as Bobbin, EI core, magnetic copper conductors, and insulating tape.
  • Sorting the materials based on the station where they are needed.
  • Assembling the primary and secondary coil windings.
  • Applying proper insulation using insulation tape.
  • Conducting insulation or continuity tests to check for any damaged or broken conductors.
  • Assembling the E and I cores.
  • Testing the voltage and continuity to ensure the transformer meets the required standard and quality.
  • Performing Vacuum Drying and Oil Painting to eliminate moisture in the transformer and use oil as an insulator and coolant.
  • Conducting Quality Analysis to ensure that the product meets the specified requirements.
  • After passing the tests, the product is packaged and shipped to the customer.

Key Learnings

Transformers are an essential element in both power systems and electronics, and they come in various sizes and configurations to fulfill the specific requirements of customers based on their usage. Manufacturing a transformer requires a meticulous process, from design to packaging. Therefore, manufacturers must always strive to maintain their high-quality standards to ensure the efficiency and reliability of their products.

Manpower is crucial in this industry since the product is manually assembled. Therefore, the company must provide a well-ventilated working environment for employees and provide them with proper PPE. However, such conditions were not observed within their factory/plant.
