GigaMare, Subic Bay Freeport Zone, Bataan

GigaMare Inc. was established on April 15, 2013, after acquiring Wärtsilä Corporation's training infrastructure in Subic and continuing its operations in the Philippines. The company's goal is to offer innovative, high-quality products and services that promote secure and efficient operations in the maritime, offshore, oil and gas, energy, and mining industries. The company provides economical and environmentally-friendly training solutions that address human factors by conducting performance gap analysis. The hands-on academy is distinctive and provides training that closely resembles real-world operation, maintenance, and repair of machinery and shipboard simulators. The company's values and workplace culture are represented in the logo, which features an image of two individuals connected together. Despite differences in size and color, the logo symbolizes the synergistic relationship between the two people.

Operation and Design

GigaMare Inc. aims to ensure dependable and efficient maritime operations and to provide skilled workforce services, and its design and operations are based on this principle. The company employs well-defined procedures to maintain the performance and durability of its assets. One of the most significant operations is OH (Overhauling), which involves meticulous inspections, repairs, and maintenance of nautical equipment to keep it in optimal condition. Additionally, GigaMare Inc. performs generator rotor pullout, a critical process that involves carefully extracting the generator rotor for repair or replacement. Through this rigorous approach, the company can maintain the equipment's prime condition, minimize downtime, and increase operational efficiency.

Procedures

The steps taken can differ based on the specific segment you are interested in. GigaMare Inc. provides three different segments, and each one may have its own unique procedures.

  • Maritime - The campus and key staff members have been located in the same spot since 2002, even before GigaMare was introduced in 2013. Numerous seafarers have enhanced their skills and over 2,000 cadets have received an advantage in their careers through the programs that were provided. To offer tangible benefits to their clients' operations, they aim to find the most effective ways to enhance the skills of their staff. They are closely working with their customers to identify their training needs and develop customized programs to fulfill those needs.
  • Energy - Since the beginning of GigaMare, the energy industry has been crucial. GigaMare has been assisting several energy sector operators through its partner in the Philippines and maintains a close relationship with ongoing power operations. GigaMare's programs empower operators and maintenance teams to carry out their routine tasks with greater safety and efficiency. Tailored management programs ensure that the necessary protocols and procedures for dependable operations are efficiently established and implemented. GigaMare provides comprehensive solutions for enhancing the skills of existing staff or ensuring the security of new installation operations.
  • Language - GigaLingua, which operates on the GigaMare campus, is a GigaMare division that specializes in language instruction. Using the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages as a basis, GigaLingua provides language instruction, currently teaching German and Finnish. GigaLingua has found that intensive in-person instruction on campus leads to higher levels of proficiency achieved in a shorter duration of time. This is exemplified by their success in training almost 300 nurses to B2 level in the German language.

Key Learnings

GigaMare Inc. is dedicated to more than just advancing their own company; they also offer a range of services to aid their clients in cultivating a competent workforce. They provide open courses, assessments, cadet programs, and certification services tailored to the industry. Their objective is to offer creative solutions that promote a safety culture and a proficient workforce, emphasizing the transformation of learning into action. This demonstrates that the company places a greater emphasis on developing people's skills than on anything else. Their aim is to make a positive difference by ensuring that they provide comprehensive training so that their workforce can have a significant impact on society. GigaMare Inc. acknowledges the advantages of co-creating course syllabi with their clients to ensure that they are effective, up-to-date, and relevant. They work closely with their clients to evaluate their staff's skills, identify competency gaps, and assess industry knowledge. They do not sever their ties with their staff once they leave the company; instead, they continue to monitor their progress to ensure that they are able to serve their clients effectively. It is evident that GigaMare Inc. prioritizes meeting their clients' needs over their own.
