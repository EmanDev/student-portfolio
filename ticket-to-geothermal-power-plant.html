Fundamentals of Power Plant Engineering Design

Power plant engineering design involves the process of designing and developing efficient and reliable systems for generating electricity from various sources of energy such as coal, natural gas, nuclear, renewable sources like solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal power. The fundamentals of power plant engineering design include the selection of appropriate technologies, equipment, and materials for the specific type of power plant, and the optimization of various factors such as efficiency, safety, cost, and environmental impact.

In this section of the portfolio, the topics discussed during our educational tour will be shown and a Question & Answer portion will be outlined which tackles questions relating to Reliability Plans and Activities.
