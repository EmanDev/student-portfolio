Webinar 1 - Fire Detection & Alarm System Fundamentals

In partnership with the Institute of Integrated Electrical Engineers, INC. (IIEE) - United Aram Emirates Chapter, the Bicol University College of Engineering - Electrical Engineering Department presents to you the Students' Industry Immersion Webinar on the topic 'Fire Detection and Alarm System Fundamentals' held on June 19, 2022 via Zoom.



Summary / Overview of the webinar

The webinar took at most 3 hours starting from 9:00AM to 12PM PHT. Engr. Liffey Jun G. Pacana, ACPE, PEE, is our speaker for this webinar. It was hosted by our beloved former block president of BSEE 3A, Ms. Cathelyn B. Baldano along with Ms. Aila A. Aranil, our emcee. This webinar wouldn't be successful if it weren't for them especially Engr. Marites E. Olesco for taking the opportunity in having this webinar for her dear students.
